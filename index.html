>> WHAT IS DEVOPS?

The Matrix has many paths. DevOps is the bridge between development and operations.

>> THE DEVOPS PHILOSOPHY

DevOps is not just tools‚Äîit's a culture, methodology, and practice that emphasizes collaboration, automation, and continuous improvement.

  • CULTURE: Breaking down silos between development and operations teams
  • AUTOMATION: Automating repetitive tasks to increase efficiency and reduce errors
  • MEASUREMENT: Monitoring and measuring everything to drive improvements
  • SHARING: Open communication and knowledge sharing across teams
  • CONTINUOUS: Continuous integration, delivery, deployment, and improvement
